Hotel Accommodations

The following hotels are holding a selection of rooms exclusively for our guests during our wedding weekend (the nights of May 3rd and 4th). The rates described below are available for booking until April 3, 2014.

We found that these hotels offer great value for a group block, but we also encourage you to explore other options. There are many wonderful places to stay and deals to be had in Austin!

Radisson Austin Downtown

111 Cesar Chavez Street, Austin, TX 78701

King or double room at $179/night.

To book, please use this special Online Reservation link, or call 1-800-333-3333 and reference the Garcia/Hutchins Room Block.

  • Beautiful hotel with fantastic river-front location
  • It’s downtown, and right next to Congress Bridge! You can walk to a plethora of fun spots (see Keep Austin Weird for our suggestions).
  • Walk across the bridge to take your trip beyond downtown: visit Auditorium Shores, stroll the Lady Bird Lake Trail, or shop at SoCo.
  • The Radisson offers a covered lot at $17/night for self-parking and $25/night for valet parking.
    • Be happy, be green! Sharing a rental car or carpooling will help with the cost and hassle of parking anywhere downtown. Please feel free to use the Guest Book to chat with fellow guests and coordinate a carpool!

Marriott Courtyard Austin South

4533 South I.H. 35, Austin, TX 78744

King room at $119/night;  double at $129;  junior suite (with sofa-bed) at $150.

To book, please use this special Online Reservation link, or call 512-912-1122 and reference the Garcia/Hutchins Room Block.

Family and bridal party recommended hotel.
Selection of rooms also available for Friday, May 2.

  • Comfortable hotel with free parking lot
  • Complimentary evening shuttle bus to downtown (from 6pm–11pm), available by making arrangements with the front desk
  • Conveniently located near US-290 for the drive to the wedding (please see Map & Directions for more details).

Alternate: The Marriott Residence Inn Austin South is immediately next door to this Courtyard hotel. Although there is no group block at the Residence Inn, it might be worth looking into for those traveling with children. The R.I. hotel brand tends to have “extended stay” rooms that are slightly larger and include kitchenettes as well as sofa-beds.
