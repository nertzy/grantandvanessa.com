Map & Directions

Click here for a Printable Map from the Camp Lucy website.

Please note: Google Maps is not a reliable source of directions for Camp Lucy. We recommend the following directions if you are driving from Austin. These directions reflect the path in pink on the map above.

  • Head west on US Hwy 290 to Dripping Springs.

  • After the signal light at RR 12 (Ranch Road 12) continue west on 290 for 4.9 miles to Holder Lane.

    • You will see green road signs along Hwy 290. Keep your eyes peeled for the “Holder Lane” sign. You should see it about 1 mile after “McGregor Lane.” If you see “Dos Lagos” or “Martin Rd” you have gone too far.

  • Turn left on Holder Lane.

  • When Holder Lane dead-ends, turn left onto Creek Road.

  • Continue on Creek Road for .9 miles until you arrive at the Camp Lucy entry, 3509 Creek Road, on your right.

  • After you enter Camp Lucy, you will see signage directing you to Ian’s Chapel as well as to Camp Lucy’s parking lot.

Would you like to carpool to Camp Lucy? Please feel free to use the Guest Book to chat with fellow guests and see if anyone wants to coordinate a car rental share.
