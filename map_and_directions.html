Map & Directions

Click here for a Printable Map from the Camp Lucy website.

Please note: Google Maps is not a reliable source of directions for Camp Lucy. We recommend the following directions if you are driving from Austin. These directions reflect the path in pink on the map above. It is about a 50‑minute drive, and we suggest that you arrive at 11am.

  • Head west on US Hwy 290 to Dripping Springs.

    • Drive towards the intersection of Hwy 290 and Ranch Road 12 (RR 12). Google Maps can get you to this intersection very easily.
    • Continue straight on Hwy 290. Immediately after you pass the signal light at RR 12, you will see a Walgreens on the left — this means you are on the right track!

  • After you pass Ranch Road 12 (RR 12), continue west on Hwy 290 for 4.9 miles to Holder Lane.

    • Holder Lane is a small road on the left side of Hwy 290. It can be easy to miss, and Google Maps gets you lost around here.
    • Look for the green road sign on the right side of Hwy 290 that points out Holder Lane. You will see the sign about 1 mile after McGregor Lane.
    • Once you see the sign for Holder Lane, the turn will be on your left, after a couple hundred feet.

  • Turn left on Holder Lane.

If you are on Hwy 290 and see a building called "PGM of Texas" on the right, you have passed Holder Lane. Find somewhere to turn around. After U-turning, you will now be heading East on Hwy 290 and will need to turn right on Holder Lane.

  • When Holder Lane dead-ends, turn left onto Creek Road.

  • Continue on Creek Road for .9 miles until you arrive at the Camp Lucy entry, 3509 Creek Road, on your right.

  • After you enter Camp Lucy, you will see signage directing you to Ian’s Chapel as well as to Camp Lucy’s parking lot.

Would you like to carpool to Camp Lucy? Please feel free to use the Guest Book to chat with fellow guests and see if anyone wants to coordinate a car rental share. Also note that it is about a $75 taxi fare (each way) from Austin.
