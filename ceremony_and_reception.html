Ceremony & Brunch Reception

We request the pleasure of your company at our wedding ceremony and reception.

Ceremony

The wedding ceremony will begin at 11:30am, inside Ian’s Chapel at Camp Lucy. A cocktail hour follows on the Great Lawn, in front of the chapel, from 12–1pm.

Brunch Reception

A fantastic brunch reception will immediately follow the cocktail hour, at 1pm.

Will there be dancing?

Yes! Following brunch, we will take our celebration to the adjacent Events Center for dancing, champagne, and cake! Our send off will be at 4pm.

What will I wear?!

Our wedding is semi-formal, “daytime chic.” The bride requests that you refrain from wearing a wedding dress. Other than that, we are sure anything you want to wear will be okay.

To enjoy lawn games during the Cocktail Hour, Stilettos may not be the way to go. For the rest of the day you will find yourself on paved ground. The brunch reception is on a courtyard with seating under a pavilion.

In May, Dripping Springs is usually warm (between 70–85 degrees). Please keep in mind that this will be a daytime event and mostly outdoors.

How will I get there?

All events will take place at Camp Lucy in Dripping Springs, Texas. It is about a 50‑minute drive from Austin, and there is free parking. We suggest that you arrive at 11am.

Camp Lucy can be tricky to find on Google Maps (and other GPS systems). Please see our Map & Directions. If you follow these directions, it will be a straight shot as well as a lovely drive in the Hill Country.
